About

Hi my name is Athena and welcome to my website. I want to create a space/ shrine to the old web. The internet as I experienced it when I was growing up. One of my earliest memories of the internet was when my mom took me to work with her one day and while she was in a meeting one of her co-workers pulled up a typical girly game site called puterdolls! My child brain was completely enthralled with the site as well as the hundreds of dress up games that the site held. I spent many a school holiday glued to my dads computer playing as many of the flash games on the site as I could. The site was my first dip into the vast ocean of the internet. Sadly the site no longer exist.
